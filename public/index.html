Well, if it isn't you

Hello, I'm Zinzy Waleson Geene, and this is my personal website. It's a space for soft stances, lived experiences, and critical notes on the things I hold dear. Learn more about me.

I write posts and updates about life, work, my interests and what I'm up to now. I treat this site like my living room, you should, too, and don't forget to check out my bookmarks. I'm growing a codex of things I believe to be true.

I'm an advocate for the IndieWeb, and I enjoy the whimsy and coziness involved in meeting Internet strangers. You are warmly invited to say hello and follow this website.
