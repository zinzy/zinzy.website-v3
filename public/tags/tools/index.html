IndieWeb Carnival: Tools

The July edition of the IndieWeb Carnival is about tools. Host James is interested in the relationship between tools and our creative practices. Remember Flickr, and the way we self-published visual sneak peeks into our lives on that website, in the mid-zeroes? If we wanted a filter in our photo, we had to Photoshop it in there ourselves.
